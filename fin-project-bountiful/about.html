About Us

Our History

We started as a small team of farmers with big ideas in a town where we grow a lot of organic and locally-grown fruits. We first established our reputation in a fruit stand along Carlsbad Beachside by providing freshly made fruit drinks and fruit bags to tourist and to the local population. As we become successful in our venture, we began to utilize the use of food trucks to cater to more people along South California Beach cities.
