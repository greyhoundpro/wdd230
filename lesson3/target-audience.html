Target Audience

The target audience of the site will be business owners who are actively looking for networking opportunities, signing up for membership in chamber, looking for events to attend, scheduling a call with the board of directors or leaders of chamber for marketing services, accessing business directories. Two personas below are examples of target audiences who are either signing up for membership or checking chamber events in their mobile phone.

Scenarios

  • I need to sign up with a membership with a chamber of commerce in my area to partner with other businesses.
  • Who are the pet store owners in my area that I can partner with?
  • I need to check the schedule of events of the chamber so I can plan ahead.
  • I would like to check other sponshorhip opportunities the chamber set for next month so I can attend.
  • What does the local chamber of commerce offer and how can I benefit?
