Alaminos Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information to the members and community of the chamber, give way for business owners to highlight their products and upcoming events. The site will also provide the audience the history of the town, current demographics, any relevant city events and information. Providing directory page or list of local businesses that are located in the area is also one of the purpose of the site. The site will also provide a way for the users to join and know the benefits of joining the chamber and the site will also give a way for the user to contact the chamber's officers and directors by filling out a form.
