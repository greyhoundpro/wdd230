Discover Alaminos City: Home of The Hundred Islands

Alaminos, Pangasinan

Alaminos City is known for being the home of the Hundred Islands National Park. The city has a total population of 99,397 according to the year 2020 census. It is also politically subdivided into 39 barangays. Alaminos City is 42 kilometres (26 mi) from Lingayen and 248 kilometres (154 mi) from Manila.

Cathedral of St. Joseph, the Patriarch

St. Joseph Cathedral Parish, most commonly known as "Alaminos Cathedral" was established on 1776. It is located in the city center of Alaminos. They celebrate the Parish Fiesta every 19th day of March.

Alaminos City Hall

Located in the City Central Business District of Alaminos, the city hall provides government services to the Alaminians with care and with passion.

Alaminos City Hundred Islands National Park

With four major developed islands: Governor's Island, Marcos Island, Children's Island, and Quezon Island, you can enjoy your island hopping adventure that includes kayaking, snorkelling, beach swimming, and camping.
